About me

STUDIES
I am in constant study and practice to continue growing both personally and professionally. Currently, I hold a degree as a Superior Technician in Programming and have obtained eight certifications in web programming. Every day, I strive to expand my knowledge in the field of information technology, staying updated with the latest trends and advancements in the sector.

HEALTH
I dedicate part of my daily time to maintaining my physical and mental health. Each day, I learn more about my body and my behaviors, identifying and becoming aware of my habits with the goal of seeking positive solutions and learning experiences. I constantly seek practical and enjoyable ways to make both mental and physical exercise a part of my lifestyle.

GASTRONOMY
I consider gastronomy an art. Currently, I specialize in creating healthy and practical meals for everyday life, such as the popular "meal preps". I apply the logic of efficiency and practicality in all aspects of my life, including the kitchen, to optimize time and resources without sacrificing quality and taste.
